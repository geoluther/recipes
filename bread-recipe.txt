No Knead Bread

3 cups flour
1/4 tsp yeast (instant, or active)
1.5 cups flower
1.5 tsp salt

mix together by hand, cover and leave on counter for 12-15 hours
preheat oven to 450-475

fold the dough on itself a few times, cover with towel and let rise for 1-2 hours.
place dutch oven, cast iron le crueset or equiv in oven to heat up.

after dough has risen again, drop in dutch oven, cover, cook for 30 mins.
the oven should be hot.

after 30 mins, remove lid and cook for 15-30 mins.
top should be brown, internal temp over 200deg.

remove, let cool a little, and eat!
